WeSharLegacy provides peace of mind with added sharing support to fill the gaps that Medicare coverage can leave, greatly reducing your potential out-of-pocket costs or medical care. Below is a snapshot of what WeShare Legacy members can expect.

WeShare Legacy® Overview

Here are just a few of the awesome benefits and resources included in your WeShare Legacy® membership:

Additional Sharing Support

Shares in eligible medical needs where Medicare leaves a gap, such as the Part A deductible, the Part B co-insurance, certain preventive services, chiropractic care, and inpatient limits.

Expanded Provider Access

If you struggle to locate a Medicare specialist within 30 miles of your home, you'll have access to the largest network of any sharing ministry through the PHCS PPO Network, with 1.2M+ doctors and specialty providers.

Preventative Health

Preventative care is shared at 100%, with no Annual Member Care Share or out-of- pocket costs.

Simple, Affordable Costs

Low monthly contributions and an Annual Member Care Share, designed to provide value while respecting your budget.
